This tool allows policymakers, researchers, and stakeholders to:

  • Explore energy performance metrics across African nations
  • Compare countries based on their readiness for energy transition
  • Analyze technological preparedness for future energy challenges
  • Identify strengths and areas for improvement in each country's energy ecosystem

Data Sources

The Africa Energy Map draws from a comprehensive range of reliable sources including national energy ministries, international organizations, academic research, and industry reports to provide the most accurate representation of the continent's energy landscape.

Scoring Framework

Each dimension is evaluated on a scale of 0-100, with higher scores indicating better performance:

  • System Performance (0-100): Measures the current efficiency, reliability, and coverage of energy systems
  • Transition Readiness (0-100): Assesses preparation for transitioning to sustainable energy sources
  • Tech Preparedness (0-100): Evaluates technological capacity for implementing modern energy solutions

Sub-Dimensions

Each main dimension is composed of multiple sub-dimensions that provide a more detailed analysis of specific aspects within the broader category. These sub-dimensions are weighted based on their relative importance to create the aggregate scores.

Limitations

While we strive for accuracy and comprehensiveness, our analysis is subject to data availability constraints. Some countries may have more complete datasets than others, which can affect comparison accuracy.
